What is Rewilding?

A revolutionary approach to conservation that lets nature lead the way

Rewilding Philosophy

3 Core Principles
"Rewilding is the process of rebuilding, following major human disturbance, a natural ecosystem by restoring natural processes and the complete or near complete food web at all trophic levels as a self-sustaining and resilient ecosystem." - Dave Foreman, Rewilding Institute

1. Core Areas

Establish large, connected wilderness areas where human impact is minimized

2. Keystone Species

Reintroduce apex predators and ecosystem engineers to restore balance

3. Natural Processes

Allow natural disturbances like fires and floods to shape landscapes
